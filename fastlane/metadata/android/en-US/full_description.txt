BoldWallet is an open-source next-generation Bitcoin wallet that eliminates traditional security tradeoffs and single points of failure.
It uses Threshold Signatures (2of2) over your mobile devices — no internet needed for setup or signing.

🔐 Features:
• ✅ Seedless setup using Threshold Signature Scheme (TSS)
• 🔐 No seed phrases, no paper backups, no hardware wallets
• 🔄 P2P encrypted protocol between two mobile devices
• 📱 2-of-2 device-based key generation and signing
• 🚫 No internet required for setup or signing
• 📦 Open-source, verifiable, and offline-capable
• 🧾 Supports Mempool.space public or self-hosted for enhanced advanced privacy
